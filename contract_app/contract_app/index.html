Smart Contracts

Smart contracts are automated computer protocols that replace traditional legal contracts, removing single points of failure and making financial agreements cheaper, faster, and more deterministic.

Smart Property

Smart property is property whose ownership is controlled via smart contracts, enabling the peer-to-peer transfer of arbitrary assets and the creation of novel financing schemes.

Smart Oracles

Smart oracles provide smart contracts with information about the outside world, serving as a provably honest source for price feeds, real-world events, and arbitration services.

Smart financial contracts. Your building blocks.

We're pioneering the development of smart financial contracts, from lending to insurance to derivatives. With an initial focus on lending, our contracts enable you to build sophisticated, modern financial services applications on the blockchain while abstracting away the underlying technical implementation details.

Smart property today. And tomorrow.

Smart property is still in its early innings, but is coming on fast. Cryptocurrencies, precious metals, and even equity are trading on the blockchain today. The Internet of Things promises to bring a plethora of smart devices onto the blockchain, and even houses and cars will trade on the blockchain in the future.
